About the Project

Kashi is not just a city; it is the spiritual capital of the world, built upon thousands of ancient temples and shrines. Unfortunately, due to the passage of time, lack of maintenance, and weathering, many of these profound architectural and spiritual landmarks are on the verge of collapse.

The Banaras Kali Bari Sansthan actively identifies heritage temples that lack resources and undertakes comprehensive structural renovations. We employ traditional artisans and stone carvers, ensuring that the historical integrity and spiritual sanctity of the original architecture are perfectly maintained.
