About the Project

Kashi is not just a city; it is the spiritual capital of the world, built upon thousands of ancient temples and shrines. Unfortunately, due to the passage of time, lack of maintenance, and weathering, many of these profound architectural landmarks are on the verge of collapse.

The Sansthan actively identifies heritage temples that lack resources and undertakes comprehensive structural renovations. We employ traditional artisans, ensuring the historical integrity of the original architecture is perfectly maintained.
