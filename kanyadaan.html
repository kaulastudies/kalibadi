Dignity & Livelihood

Women Empowerment & Kanyadaan

Facilitating respectful marriages for daughters of impoverished families and fostering financial independence through micro-enterprises.

About the Project

In deeply impoverished communities, the marriage of a daughter can force a family into generational debt. Our Kanyadaan initiative steps in to take full financial and moral responsibility for these marriages. We ensure that girls from destitute backgrounds are married with dignity, respect, and the essential resources needed to start their new lives. Our next major Kanyadaan event is scheduled in less than a month.

Beyond marriage, true empowerment comes from financial independence. We are actively setting up micro-enterprise units—including tailoring centers, basic education classes, and pickle-making cooperatives—enabling women to earn a sustainable livelihood and support their families.

Our Mission & Impact

  • ✓ Kanyadaan Support: Funding venue, clothing, food, and essential household items for the newlyweds.
  • ✓ Tailoring Units: Providing sewing machines and training to local women.
  • ✓ Micro-Enterprises: Establishing cottage industries (like pickle manufacturing) to generate steady income.
